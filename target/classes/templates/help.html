Metro Services Help Center

Ticket Booking Instructions

1. Navigate to Ticket Booking: Click on the "Book Ticket" button to access the ticket booking page.

2. Select Inputs: Choose your source and destination stations from the dropdown menus.

3. Choose Date and Time: Select your preferred travel date and time.

4. Enter Details: Fill out the required passenger information fields.

5. Review and Confirm: Double-check your details and confirm your booking by clicking the "Confirm" button.

Route Details Instructions

1. Navigate to Route Details: Click on the "View Route Details" button to access the route details page.

2.Select Input: Choose your source and destination stations from the dropdown menus.

3. View Route Info: Once selected, you will see detailed route information, including stops, distances, and estimated travel time.

4. Explore Route Alternatives: You can explore alternative routes by adjusting your source and destination stations.

Instructions for Reporting Lost Items

1. Verify Availability: Before reporting a lost item, please check the "Found Items" table below to see if your item has been reported as found by another user.

2. Complete the Form: If you do not find your lost item in the "Found Items" table, please fill out the form below with accurate details about your lost item.

3. Provide Detailed Information: Include as much detail as possible about the lost item, including its name, description, and where it was last seen. Providing detailed information will help in identifying and locating the lost item.

4. Contact Information: Make sure to provide valid contact information so that we can reach out to you if your lost item is found.

5. Submit Form: Once you have filled out the form, click the "Submit" button to send the information. Our team will review your submission and get in touch with you if we have any updates regarding your lost item.

Instructions for Using Fare Calculator

2. Calculate Fare: Click on the "Calculate Fare" button to determine the fare for the journey.

Instructions for Map Section

1. Explore the map to discover nearby attractions and landmarks.

2. Use the search functionality to find specific locations within the map.

3. Download the map for offline access by clicking the "Download" button.

Instructions for Station Search

1. Enter the name of the station you want to search for in the input field.

2. Click the "Search" button to find the station within the map.
