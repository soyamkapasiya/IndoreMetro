Places to visit in Indore

Gandhi Hall

Near the historic Gandhi Hall, a landmark known for its architectural grandeur and cultural significance

Rajwada

Close to Rajwada, a magnificent palace reflecting the rich history and architectural heritage of Indore.

Lal Bagh

Adjacent to Lal Bagh Palace, a stunning royal residence showcasing a blend of European and Indian architectural styles.

Annapurna

Near the revered Annapurna Temple, a spiritual hub dedicated to the Hindu goddess of nourishment and abundance

Chokhi Dhani

Conveniently located for access to Chokhi Dhani, an ethnic village resort offering traditional Rajasthani culture, cuisine, and entertainment.

Khajrana

Close to Khajrana Ganesh Temple, a popular Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, attracting devotees and tourists alike.

Meghdoot Garden

Adjacent to Meghdoot Garden, a picturesque botanical garden featuring lush greenery, colorful flowers, and serene water bodies.

Patalpan

Near Patalpani Waterfall, a scenic natural attraction known for its cascading waters and picturesque surroundings

Ralamandal

Conveniently located for access to Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary, a nature reserve offering diverse flora and fauna in a serene environment.

Tincha Falls

Close to Tincha Falls, a mesmerizing waterfall nestled amidst lush greenery, offering a refreshing escape into nature.

Indore Zoo

Adjacent to Indore Zoo, a popular destination housing a variety of wildlife species, providing educational and recreational experiences for visitors
