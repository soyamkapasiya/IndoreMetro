Frequently Asked Questions

Ans: The Indore Metro is a rapid transit system serving the city of Indore, India. It aims to provide a convenient and efficient mode of public transportation for residents and visitors.

Ans: You can purchase tickets for the Indore Metro at ticket counters located at metro stations. Additionally, automated ticket vending machines are available at some stations for self-service ticket purchase.

Ans: The Indore Metro operates from early morning until late at night, typically from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. However, please note that the operating hours may vary on holidays and special occasions.

Ans: Yes, senior citizens and students are eligible for discounted fares on the Indore Metro. They may need to provide valid identification or student cards to avail of these discounts.

Ans: Luggage is allowed on the Indore Metro, but passengers are encouraged to keep their belongings to a minimum to ensure a comfortable journey for everyone. Pets are generally not allowed on the metro, except for service animals accompanying passengers with disabilities.

Ans: Trains on the Indore Metro run at regular intervals throughout the day, with higher frequencies during peak hours and reduced frequencies during off-peak hours. The exact schedule may vary depending on the day of the week and other factors.

Ans: Yes, the Indore Metro is designed to be accessible to passengers with disabilities. Stations are equipped with elevators, ramps, and designated spaces for wheelchair users. Additionally, staff members are trained to assist passengers with special needs.

Ans: If you lose an item on the Indore Metro, you can contact the Lost and Found department at the nearest metro station. They will assist you in locating and retrieving your lost item, if possible.
